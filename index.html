Meet Curtis.

Curtis is a graphics system that generates beautiful visuals for any arbitrary set of MIDI-files.

It offers a variety of visualization methods, and allows for easy creation and customizability to fit your music.

The visualizations can be generated as-is (in any arbitrary video format), or displayed in real time with inputs coming from your MIDI-device/keyboard.

It is also heavily customizable, with practically limitless opportunities.

Curtis can also generate visualizations independent from your music. The tempo can still be adjusted to fit your music!
